Farmer Martin is driving his tractor along the village road. He has a trailer load of fertilizer. Bobby a boy of nine is playing near the house when he notices this man and asks, “What have you got in this trailer?”

“Manure”, answers English farmer.

“What are you planning to do with it?” asks Bobby.

“Put it on my strawberries”, answers Martin.

Bobby says, “You should come and eat with us, we usually put ice-cream on our strawberries”.
